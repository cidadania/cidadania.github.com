Welcome to Cidadania Open Source Labs

Here is where we store all our open source projects so everyone can enjoy them using the tools provided by GitHub. We also have another repos, but this is the easiest to use for everyone.

Enjoy your stay, and if you are interested in a project, feel free to download it, clone the repo, modify anything and redistribute it!. Also, if you have made some patches on your own we would like to see them, every contribution if for the community.

What is this page?

This webpage is the main site of our GitHub repository, where we host all out open source projects. Here you can find the project index and some other links.

What is Cidadania?

Cidadania is a cooperative company formed by professionals of the social sciences with orientation to the social usefulness of the investigations and interventions. Innovative character in the area of the civil participation and the participative democracies.

Founded in 1999, more than ten years of Cidadania it is employed at the community and public dimension: public administration, social economy, associative sector, social movements...
